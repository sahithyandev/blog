## Mathematics/Science related channels

#### [3Blue1Brown](__YOUTUBE_CHANNEL__/UCYO_jab_esuFRV4b17AJtAw)

3Blue1Brown, by Grant Sanderson, is some combination of math and entertainment, depending on your disposition. The goal is for explanations to be driven by animations and for difficult problems to be made simple with changes in perspective.

He explains Mathematical concepts in an easy-to-understand way using awesome visualisations.

To learn more about 3Blue1Brown, visit their [website](https://www.3blue1brown.com/).
